# JTBD Interview Script — Healthcare / Service Business

## Pre-Interview Setup

- Duration: 20–30 minutes
- Record with permission ("So I can focus on listening, not note-taking")
- Remind: no right or wrong answers, you want honest experience
- Don't mention your product until they bring it up

---

## The Script

### Opening (2 min)
"Thank you so much for your time. I want to understand your experience — specifically what was going on in your life when you first decided to look for help with [problem area]. There are no right or wrong answers. I'm mostly going to listen and ask a few follow-up questions."

---

### Block 1: The Problem (5 min)

**Q1.1** — "When did you first notice [the problem]? What was happening in your life at that time?"
- Probe: "Can you describe what it felt like?"
- Probe: "How long had it been going on before you started looking for help?"

**Q1.2** — "How was it affecting your day-to-day life?"
- Probe: "What couldn't you do that you used to be able to do?"
- Probe: "Was there a specific moment that made you think 'I need to do something about this'?"

**Q1.3** — "Did you try to solve it on your own before seeking professional help? What did you try?"
- Probe: "What made you think that would work?"
- Probe: "What happened when you tried it?"

---

### Block 2: The Search (5 min)

**Q2.1** — "When you decided to actively look for a solution, what did you do first?"
- Probe: "What words did you use when you searched online?"
- Probe: "Did you ask anyone for a recommendation?"

**Q2.2** — "What were you comparing? What other options did you consider?"
- Probe: "What would you have done if this option hadn't existed?"

**Q2.3** — "What almost stopped you from going ahead?"
- Probe: "Were you worried about anything?"
- Probe: "Was there anything that nearly made you choose something else?"

---

### Block 3: The Decision (5 min)

**Q3.1** — "What made you choose [this solution / clinic / service] specifically?"
- Probe: "Was it something someone said? Something you read?"
- Probe: "Did reviews or recommendations influence you?"

**Q3.2** — "What did you expect to happen after your first appointment?"

**Q3.3** — "What would have made you feel like you'd made a bad decision?"

---

### Block 4: Outcome & Success (5 min)

**Q4.1** — "What does success look like for you? How will you know this has worked?"
- Probe: "Is there a specific thing you want to be able to do again?"
- Probe: "How will you feel when you get there?"

**Q4.2** — "What's the first thing you'll do when [problem] is resolved?"
*(This reveals the hidden desire — the fantasy they're actually buying.)*

**Q4.3** — "If a friend was going through the same thing, what would you tell them?"
*(This is testimonial gold — note verbatim.)*

---

### Closing (2 min)
"That's everything — thank you. One last question: is there anything I didn't ask that you think would be useful for me to understand?"

---

## Analysis Notes

After the interview, immediately note:
- The single strongest emotional quote
- The trigger event (in their exact words)
- The success criteria (in their exact words)
- Any phrase that surprised you

These are your headline candidates.
